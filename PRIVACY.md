# Privacy Policy — Vibesfolio

**Last updated: March 19, 2026**

## Overview

Vibesfolio ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome extension that lets users save prompts and web resources to their Vibesfolio library. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected.

## What data we collect

### Authentication information
When you sign in, we store your Supabase authentication tokens (access token and refresh token) locally in `chrome.storage.local`. These are used solely to keep you signed in to the extension.

### Personally identifiable information
Your email address is associated with your Vibesfolio account and stored in Supabase. We do not collect your name, phone number, or any other personal details beyond what you provide during sign-up.

### Website content you choose to save
When you save a resource, the extension reads the active tab's URL and page title to pre-fill the save form. This data is only sent to Supabase when you explicitly click "Save to Vault". We do not read or transmit any other content from pages you visit.

When you save a prompt via right-click, only the text you selected is captured — nothing else from the page.

## What data we do NOT collect

- We do not track your browsing history
- We do not collect data from pages you visit but do not save
- We do not use analytics or advertising trackers
- We do not sell your data to third parties

## How data is stored

- **Locally:** Auth tokens are stored in `chrome.storage.local` on your device
- **Remotely:** Saved prompts, resources, and your account information are stored in Supabase (supabase.com), our database provider

## Third-party services

- **Supabase** (supabase.com) — authentication and database. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at tanya@vibesfolio.app or through the settings in the Vibesfolio web app.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
